Akhilesh Khot

Short Bio: Akhilesh Khot holds a bachelor's degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering
from Savitribai Phule University (SPPU). His research interests encompass Quantum Communication,
Quantum Information Theory, and Integrated Circuit Design. Presently, he is engaged in the development
of an embedded system for FMCW Radar applications. He possesses a high level of proficiency in
mathematics, particularly in the field of Linear Algebra.
